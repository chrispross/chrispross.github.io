Another sample post from 2025 to show how the year accordion works.

When you click on a year in the Writing archive, it expands to show posts grouped by month. This post is from November 2025, so it will appear under that month.

## How to Add New Posts

1. Create a new file in the `_posts` folder
2. Name it `YYYY-MM-DD-your-post-title.md`
3. Add the front matter (title, description, date)
4. Write your content in Markdown

That's it. Jekyll handles the rest.
